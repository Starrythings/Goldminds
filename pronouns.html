Pronouns

Pronouns are words used to replace nouns in a sentence. They can be personal, possessive, reflexive, etc.

  • I, You, He, She, It: Personal pronouns
  • My, Your, His, Her: Possessive pronouns
  • Myself, Yourself: Reflexive pronouns
